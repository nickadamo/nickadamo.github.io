About

Scott Shepard began his career as a cost analyst for a FORTUNE 100 manufacturing company and learned the intricacies of large organizations through his work in corporate accounting and finance.

Entertainment Law

Our attorneys blend expertise in copyright and trademark law, transactional negotiations, privacy and publicity rights, production and licensing agreements, with experience in film, music, television, theater, publishing and advertising. The result is effective legal representation for clients on both the creative and business side of the industry, which allows our clients to focus more of their time on what they do best.

Business

Businesses, whether large or small, established or just starting up, need business-savvy attorneys who can help grow and protect their livelihood. We aim to understand our clients’ needs and goals in order to further their success and growth, while anticipating and protecting our clients from issues that may arise in the future, even beyond traditional risk management. Our legal solutions help to ensure efficiency, achieve goals and protect profitability..

Litigation

We represent our clients in formal litigation and dispute resolution, backed by aggressive, high-caliber advocacy. We work to establish a thoughtful endgame, and zealously represent our clients’ interests, while avoiding unnecessary litigation. As trial attorneys we enjoy the challenge of the courtroom. Our approach to civil litigation, whether common or complex, is built on focused preparation, non-linear thinking and efficient strategies..
